FAQ

We have the answers

We specialize in concrete work (driveways, steps, patios, walkways, garage and basement floors), natural stone installations (flagstone, armor stone, garden walls), interlocking stone features (retaining walls, patios, driveways), and full hardscaping services including design, grading, sodding and planting.

Each material has its advantages. Concrete is cost-effective and highly durable, interlocking stone offers design flexibility and easy repair, while natural stone delivers a premium, timeless look. We'll guide you in selecting the best option based on your budget, style and intended use.

Yes. We offer complete outdoor solutions — combining hardscaping (patios, driveways and stonework) with softscaping (sodding, planting and grading) to create a cohesive, beautiful space from start to finish.

Most projects are completed within a few days to a couple of weeks depending on size and complexity. While we can assist with guidance, clients are typically responsible for obtaining any necessary permits. We're happy to advise you through the process.

Yes. We stand behind our work with a workmanship warranty and only use quality materials to ensure long-term performance. Warranty details are provided during the project consultation.
